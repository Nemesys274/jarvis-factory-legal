Privacy Policy — Jarvis Factory

Last updated: 5 May 2026

Scope

Jarvis Factory is a single-user personal automation tool used by its operator (Etienne Reclus) to manage their own TikTok creator account workflow. It is not a service offered to third parties, has no other users, and does not collect data from anyone other than its operator.

Data accessed via TikTok APIs

When the operator authenticates with TikTok via Login Kit, the application may access:

  • The operator's TikTok user profile (display name, avatar, open ID).
  • The operator's own videos and their public metrics (views, likes, comments).
  • The ability to publish drafts and scheduled videos to the operator's own account, via the Content Posting API.

The application only accesses the operator's own TikTok account. It does not access, collect, or process data from other users.

Storage

Tokens and metadata are stored locally on the operator's personal computer in encrypted form. Nothing is shared with third parties. No data is transmitted to external servers under the operator's control.

Sharing

No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party. The application has no advertising, no analytics, no telemetry.

Retention

Data is kept locally for as long as the operator uses the application. The operator can revoke access at any time via TikTok account settings, which immediately invalidates the application's tokens.
